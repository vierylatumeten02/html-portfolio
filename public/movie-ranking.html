My top 3 movie

Don't Look Up

Very sarcastic film, but they try to tell meaningful messages about human on this ages, and what it'll be on future if we not change.

The Wolf of Wall Street

Really good visualisation about the character and how it evolve and become another person at the end of the movie, and it's show our deepest desire as human being, with money, popularity, freedom, etc.

Social Dilemma

A documentary that shows how social media impacting our society, what it can do, how it works on the background and we are not aware about it. It's show us a reflection about our behavior this ages, we are depends on our phone.
